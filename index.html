Creativity

I define it as the tendency to generate or recognize ideas, alternatives, or possibilities that may be useful in solving problems, communicating with others, and entertaining ourselves and others. I use creativity in everything I do.

Professionalism

Professionalism involves being reliable, setting your own high standards, and showing that you care about every aspect of your job. It's about being industrious and organized, and holding yourself accountable for your thoughts, words and actions.

Technique

A technique is a particular method of doing an activity, usually a method that involves practical skill. and ability in an artistic, sporting, or other practical activity that I developed through training and practice.

Experience
